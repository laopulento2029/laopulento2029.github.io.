Pampanga Landmarks

Aqua Planet

One of the largest water parks,located in Clark Freeport Zone and it features a wide variety of water attractions, including wave pools, slides, lazy rivers, and kiddie pools. It’s a popular destination for families and friends looking to cool off and have fun, especially during hot weather or holidays.

Puning Hot Spring

A natural geothermal spring near Mount Pinatubo. The site features multiple stations, including a base camp with a restaurant, a sand spa where visitors can lie in warm volcanic sand or try volcanic mud packs, and hot spring pools for soaking. Enjoy warm volcanic pools, sand spas, and mud packs after a scenic 4×4 ride.

Betis Church

Known as the “Sistine Chapel of the Philippines” for its stunning ceiling murals and ornate Baroque decorations. Showcasing Pampanga’s rich artistic heritage through intricate paintings and wood carvings. Check out nearby Betis workshops for hand-carved furniture and religious statues which reflects the amazing craftsmanship of the locals.

Sky Ranch Pampanga

A fun amusement park in San Fernando featuring rides, a giant Ferris wheel, and stunning city views. Perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike.

Nayong Pilipino Clark

A cultural theme park showcasing Filipino traditions, architecture, and folk performances. A quick tour through the country’s heritage in one spot.

CDC Parade Grounds

A wide open field in Clark used for jogging, biking, and night markets. It's a popular spot for morning runs, workuts, and weekend hangouts thanks to its spacious, relaxing, and "food trip" experience!

Aurora Landmarks

Ditumabo Mother Falls

Also known as Mother Falls, this majestic waterfall in Dingalan, Aurora is surrounded by lush forest and rugged terrain. Visitors trek through scenic trails to reach its powerful cascades and natural pools, offering a refreshing and invigorating experience for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

View on Map

Dicasalarin Cove

Located in Baler, Aurora, Dicasalarin Cove is a quiet beach with soft white sand, clear blue waters, and tall cliffs around it. It’s a great place to swim, relax, have a picnic, or just enjoy the view of the Pacific Ocean. The peaceful setting makes it perfect for taking photos or getting away from the busy town.

View on Map

Batanes of the East

Dubbed the “Batanes of the East,” the coastal landscapes of Dingalan, Aurora feature rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, and panoramic ocean views reminiscent of the northernmost islands of the Philippines. It’s a favorite spot for photographers, hikers, and travelers seeking peaceful, untouched scenery.

View on Map

Dinadiawan Beach

Found in Sabang, Baler, Dinadiawan Beach has a long stretch of golden sand and gentle waves. It’s perfect for swimming, surfing, or just walking along the shore. The beach is lined with coconut trees that provide shade, making it a great spot for families or friends to relax and enjoy a day by the sea.

View on Map

Aniao Islet

Just off the coast of Baler, Aurora, Aniao Islet is a small island with clear water and colorful fish, perfect for snorkeling or swimming. The soft sandy shore is ideal for relaxing and enjoying the sun. Its calm and quiet surroundings make it a great place to spend a peaceful day away from the crowd..

Diguisit Beach

A serene beach in Baler with soft sand and gentle waves. Great for swimming, picnics, or simply soaking up the quiet seaside vibe.
