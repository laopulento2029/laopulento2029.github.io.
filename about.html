Game Mechanics Board

Overview

Survive the school year by completing maze challenges and answering quizzes. Manage your stats wisely and reach Final Exam Day to win.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to navigate the maze
  • Reach the goal (G) to complete the level
  • Answer quiz questions correctly
  • Balance Energy, Knowledge, and Stress

Win Condition

Reach Final Exam Day with sufficient Knowledge level.

Lose Condition

Energy reaches 0 or Stress reaches 100.

Cheat Sheet

Prioritize Energy. Reduce Stress early. Knowledge is key.

Review these subjects: Geography, Chem, Math, General Knowledge, Biology. No one said you couldn't use Google or Safari
